Welcome to Tennis Legend!

Providing all the essential Grand Slam Tennis Tournament information, and opportunities to watch live tennis in the most exciting cities in the world

The Grand Slam Tennis Tournaments are the world's most important and prestigious professional tennis tournaments. There are four held each year, offering the most ranking points, prize money, attention from the public and media, and have the greatest strength and size of player field.

Total ranking points offered to the winner of a Grand Slam are 2,000. This is by far the most of all the tournaments held throughout the year. They also hold the longest matches for men, which are best of 5 sets. Matches at these tournaments can go on for as long as 5 hours.

Each Grand Slam has competitions for both men and women in the singles and doubles, as well as mixed doubles and junior (U18) championships.
